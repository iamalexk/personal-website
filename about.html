About

Alex Kim

Alex is a junior at the University of Michigan studying computer science and business administration. He hopes to combine his passion for technology, business, and design through a career in product management or software engineering.

In addition to his studies, Alex is a teaching assistant for EECS 183 and EECS 280-two required courses for those studying computer science, computer engineering, or electrical engineering at the University of Michigan.

Outside of the classroom, Alex enjoys playing sports such as soccer, basketball, and golf. As a starting defender for the University of Michigan Men's Club Soccer team, he helped bring the club a Midwest Alliance Soccer Conference Regional Championship and two final eight national tournament appearances.
